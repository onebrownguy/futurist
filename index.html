Office for the Future

Austin Community College’s Office for the Future is where civic leadership, institutional innovation, and community investment come together - building the workforce, the technology ecosystem, and the educational infrastructure that Austin needs to thrive for generations to come.

Led by Executive Vice Chancellor Dr. Monique Reeves, the Office for the Future positions ACC as more than a college - as a strategic civic partner for the City of Austin, Travis County, and the broader Central Texas region. We work alongside industry leaders, municipal stakeholders, philanthropic organizations, and community advocates to ensure that ACC’s work is directly responsive to the needs of the people we serve.

A Hub for AI & Emerging Technology
Through the Austin AI Alliance, the Digital Twin Consortium®, and the Digital Education Council, ACC is at the center of Austin’s artificial intelligence and emerging technology ecosystem. These partnerships connect local employers, city agencies, and academic institutions to prepare Austin’s workforce for the industries shaping our economy - today and in the decades ahead.

Investing in Entrepreneurship & Economic Mobility
The Entrepreneurship & Innovation Academy and our student-facing programs like NEST FEST are creating a pipeline of entrepreneurial talent rooted in Austin’s most underrepresented communities. We believe that economic mobility begins with access - and that ACC students deserve a clear, supported pathway from the classroom to the boardroom.

Transforming How Education Is Delivered
Transform_Ed and the Riverbat Experience initiative are reimagining what a community college education looks like in the 21st century - modernizing instruction, removing structural barriers, and ensuring that ACC’s 80,000+ students receive an education that is equitable, excellent, and built for the future.

Research, Foresight & Civic Leadership
The Office for the Future conducts applied research, cultivates national partnerships, and engages civic and philanthropic stakeholders to ensure that ACC’s long-range strategy is informed by the best available evidence - and aligned with the long-term interests of our community.

We invite city officials, community organizations, and regional partners to engage with the work of the Office for the Future. Whether through partnership, sponsorship, advisory participation, or advocacy, there is a meaningful role for every stakeholder who believes in the power of accessible, innovative higher education to transform lives and strengthen Austin.
